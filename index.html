Introduction

Image style learning

This work is inspired by the work from A Neural Algorithm of Artistic Style. In this final project, we will reproduce the work produced in A Neural Algorithm of Artistic Style and and adapt to other kind of images to see the potential of this algorithm.

In A Neural Algorithm of Artistic Style, the authors only applied the algorithm on fine art images. We aim to apply the same algorithm proposed here to other kind of images such as photographs and, if times permit, frames in cinematography.

The outline of this blog will follows topics in the following order:

  1. Reproduction of A Neural Algorithm of Artistic Style
  2. Apply on other kinds of images
  3. Future Works
